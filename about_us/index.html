About Us

Who are we?

We are a dedicated team of people passionate about potential unlocked by bleeding edge technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine learning, Blockchain, Augmented & Virtual Reality. We believe that these technologies in combination with the Internet of Things will profoundly change the way we percieve and interact with the world around us.

AI, Blockchain, AR and IoT will usher in a new way of life and will unlock the next post-industrial revolution

So what does all this actually mean

AI and ML today allow us to analyse data at scale, derive insights and predict the future with more reliability than ever before.
Blockchain is a revolutionary technology that takes away centralized control of data and allows transparency at levels never before possible.
Augmented and virtual reality provides us the tools to create interfaces / user experiences that are more intutive, so that we can leverage these complex technologies and use them to make our daily lives better.
The Internet of Things will allow seamless communication with every object in our world.

All the above, combined, will allow us the level of sophistication and control that has only been possible in our dreams and worlds of only the most powerful magicians.


That is our vision. We strive hard everyday to bring it one step closer to reality.
